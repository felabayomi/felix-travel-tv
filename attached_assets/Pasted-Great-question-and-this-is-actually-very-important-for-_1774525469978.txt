# Travel Content Style Options (For Felix Travel TV)

## 1. Documentary Style (Discovery Channel / National Geographic)

**Tone:** Calm, cinematic, informative, storytelling

**Example script style:**

> Welcome to Chicago, a city known for its skyline, deep-dish pizza, and rich architectural history.
> Located along Lake Michigan, Chicago offers visitors world-class museums, waterfront parks, and vibrant neighborhoods full of culture and food.

**Use this for:**

* City Discovery
* Expedition America
* Destination features
* History
* Culture
* Scenic videos

**Feels like:** National Geographic, Discovery Channel

---

## 2. Travel Show Host Style (This is VERY good for you)

**Tone:** Friendly, energetic, like a TV host guiding viewers

**Example:**

> Today on Felix Travel TV, we’re heading to Chicago, one of the best cities in America for food, architecture, and city experiences.
> I’ll show you where to stay, what to eat, what to see, and how to plan your trip the smart way.

**Use this for:**

* Felix Travel TV main content
* Travel planning
* Travel tips
* Where to stay
* Before you book
* City guides

**Feels like:** Travel Channel host, YouTube travel host

---

## 3. Travel Advisor Style (Expert / Consultant)

**Tone:** Professional, helpful, advisory

**Example:**

> If you’re planning a trip to Chicago, here are a few things you should know before booking your hotel.
> The best areas to stay are downtown, River North, and Magnificent Mile.
> Hotel prices vary depending on the season, so it’s important to plan ahead.

**Use this for:**

* Before You Book
* Travel tips
* Travel mistakes
* Booking advice
* Flights, hotels, insurance
* Visa tips
* Planning videos

---

## 4. News / Update Style (Travel News Anchor)

**Tone:** Informational, updates, alerts

**Example:**

> In travel news today, several airlines have announced new routes for summer travel, and hotel prices in major U.S. cities are expected to rise due to increased demand.

**Use this for:**

* Real-Time Travel Advisor
* Travel alerts
* Travel news
* Airline updates
* Visa updates
* Travel warnings

---

# The Smart Strategy (Very Important)

You should **NOT use only one style.**

**Felix Travel TV should have different styles for different segments.**

| Segment               | Style                   |
| --------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Felix Travel TV Intro | Travel Show Host        |
| City Discovery        | Documentary             |
| Expedition America    | Documentary / Adventure |
| Before You Book       | Travel Advisor          |
| Travel Tips           | Travel Advisor          |
| Travel News           | News Anchor             |
| Where To Next         | Travel Show Host        |
| Done For You Travel   | Travel Advisor          |
| Travel Deals          | Host / Advisor          |
| Hotel Reviews         | Host                    |
| City Tours            | Documentary             |

This makes your channel feel like a **real TV network**.
