That looks really good actually — your News Reader waiting screen already looks like something a real broadcast would use.

Now the important part:
When it says “Waiting for the broadcast to begin”, that screen should give viewers useful information so they don’t leave.

Think of it like a TV channel before the news starts.

⸻

What To Show On “Waiting For Broadcast” Screen

Here are the best things to include:

1. Countdown Timer (Most Important)

Add:

Broadcast begins in 14:32

This keeps people watching because they know when it starts.

⸻

2. Today’s Topics / Headlines

Add a small section like:

Today's Topics:
• Travel Industry Updates
• Flight Deals of the Week
• New Visa Rules
• Travel Tech Tools
• City of the Day
• Finance & Travel Costs

This tells viewers what they will get.

⸻

3. Your Brand / Channel Info

Add something like:

City Discoverer Live
Travel Planning • Deals • Tools • News
Live Travel Office


⸻

4. Social / Website / Booking

Since you’re also a travel planner, this is very important:

Add:

Need help planning a trip?
Book a session:
globaltravelplanner.com

Follow:
Twitch.tv/globaltravelplanner
YouTube: City Discoverer

This turns your stream into marketing automatically.

⸻

5. Scrolling News Ticker (You Already Have This)

The ticker at the bottom is perfect.
You can use it for:
	•	Travel news
	•	Flight deals
	•	Travel tips
	•	Exchange rates
	•	Headlines
	•	Announcements

Examples:

Flight deals this week: NYC → Paris $420
Passport processing delays increasing
